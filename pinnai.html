Smart maintenance (CMMS + AI)

Your maintenance is reactive. Something breaks, you scramble. Techs waste time on paperwork. Parts are either overstocked or missing when you need them most.

With PinnAI: Preventive maintenance schedules itself. Work orders flow automatically to the right technician. Parts inventory tracks itself. And AI learns your machine patterns to predict failures weeks before they happen.

Automated quality control

Paper inspection forms are slowing you down. Data entry errors, lost reports, clients asking for documentation you can't find fast enough. Your quality process works, but it doesn't scale.

With PinnAI: Digital inspection with photo capture right on the floor. SPC charts update in real time. Non-conformances trigger automatic workflows. Client quality reports generate themselves.

AI that works for you

Artificial intelligence isn't just for tech giants. We make it work for your reality.

AI Assistants

Your team spends 2 hours a day searching for information. Digging through shared drives, emailing colleagues, flipping through binders. The answer exists — they just can't find it fast enough.

Imagine a chatbot that knows your entire product catalog, maintenance history, and every technical document you've ever created. Your team asks in plain language — "What's the torque spec for valve model X?" — and gets the answer instantly. Trained on YOUR data. Accurate. Available 24/7.

Process Automation

Your AP team processes 50 invoices a day by hand. Copy-paste from emails to the ERP. Match POs. Chase approvals. It's tedious, error-prone, and costs you real money in labor.

What if that 2-hour daily grind took 10 minutes? AI reads invoices, matches them to purchase orders, routes approvals, and enters everything into your system. Same for onboarding, expense reports, compliance checks — any repetitive process with decisions. Automated intelligently.

Document Intelligence

You have 10 years of engineering drawings sitting in a filing cabinet. Inspection reports in PDF. Safety docs in Word. Technical specs in Excel. All valuable. All unsearchable.

AI extracts the data from every document — drawings, reports, specs, contracts. Organizes it. Makes it searchable. Need to find every project where you used a specific material? 3 seconds. That filing cabinet just became your most valuable database.

Voice-to-Data

Your field tech finishes an inspection and now has to sit in the truck for 30 minutes filling out forms. Half the details get lost. Handwriting is illegible. The report arrives 3 days late.

Instead: they talk into their phone for 5 minutes. AI transcribes it, extracts every data point, fills out the inspection form, attaches the photos, flags issues, and sends the report — all before they start the truck. Field work stays in the field. Paperwork does itself.

Predictive Maintenance

That CNC machine just went down unexpectedly. Again. The repair takes 6 hours. The parts are on backorder. You missed a delivery deadline. The cost? $15,000 in lost production — for a bearing that costs $200.

Your machines generate data 24/7 — vibration, temperature, pressure, current. AI watches all of it and tells you which machine needs attention BEFORE it breaks. "Replace bearing on Machine 7 within 2 weeks." That's the difference between a $200 planned repair and a $15,000 emergency. Reduce unplanned downtime by 30-50%.

AI Agents

Your managers spend half their day on tasks a machine could do better. Compiling reports, monitoring KPIs, checking inventory levels, following up on overdue items. Important work — but not the best use of their brain.

Deploy AI agents that work 24/7: monitoring your systems, spotting anomalies, generating daily reports, sending alerts when something needs attention. Think of them as tireless digital team members who never miss a detail, never take a sick day, and never forget to follow up.
